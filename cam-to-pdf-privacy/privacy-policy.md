# Privacy Policy for Cam to PDF

**Last updated: May 29, 2026**

## Overview

Cam to PDF ("the App") is a mobile application that allows users to capture images using their camera or photo library and convert them into PDF documents. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Camera images**: Photos you take or select from your photo library are processed locally on your device to create PDF files. These images are not uploaded to any server.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Advertising identifier (IDFA)**: With your consent, we may collect your device's advertising identifier to deliver personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.

## How We Use Your Information

- Camera images and documents are processed **entirely on your device** and are never transmitted to external servers.
- The advertising identifier may be used by Google AdMob to show relevant ads.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

AdMob may collect:
- Advertising identifiers
- Device information
- Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for this app
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Data Storage

All documents and PDF files created by the App are stored locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any files you create.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Camera | Capture documents and images for PDF conversion |
| Photo Library | Select existing images for PDF conversion |
| App Tracking Transparency (iOS) | Request consent before using advertising identifier |

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
